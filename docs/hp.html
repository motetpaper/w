MOTET HANYUPINYIN

Convert Chinese characters to Hanyu Pinyin with tone numbers in Google Sheets.

About MOTET HANYUPINYIN

MOTET HANYUPINYIN is a Google Sheets custom function that converts Chinese characters to Hanyu Pinyin annotation with tone numbers.

SYNOPSIS

=MOTET_HANYUPINYIN(text) - text is the only parameter for this function. Chinese characters are expected as input. The output is a corresponding space-delimited list of Hanyu Pinyin (toneme) sounds.
